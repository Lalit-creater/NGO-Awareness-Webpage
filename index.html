About Us

A Foundation Built on Trust and Purpose

InAmigos Foundation is licensed by the Central Government and registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013. Founded on 23 September 2020, we operate from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, with a single guiding mission.

"Uniting Minds For Change."

  • Licensed by Central Government
  • Registered under Section 8, Companies Act 2013
  • Founded 23 September 2020
  • Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh
